About Us

We’re a small Inner West studio that builds websites that actually work

We’re a small Inner West studio that builds websites that give you business

Hatch Studio is a family-owned web and SEO studio based in Sydney’s Inner West.
We help local cafés, tradies and service businesses get a website and online presence that doesn’t just look pretty – it actually shows up on Google and brings in real enquiries.

Our Mission​

Our mission is simple:
help local Inner West and Canterbury businesses get a web presence that pulls its weight.
That means:
– Websites that are fast, clear and easy to use on mobile.
– Copy that tells your story and makes it obvious why someone should choose you.
– SEO baked in from day one so you can be found when people search for what you do.
If your website isn’t helping you win work, we see that as a problem to solve – not “just how it is”.
